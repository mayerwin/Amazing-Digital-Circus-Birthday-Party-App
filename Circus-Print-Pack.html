How to print: Print this on A4, colour, 100% scale. Each section starts on a new page. Pages: (1–2) character badges, (3–6) the four station signs, (7) the Exit Door sign, (8) burger pieces. Cut the badges out and stick them on the kids during the reveal. Tape the signs at each station. The Exit Door card shows the colour code the kids tap on the iPad. ◉‿◉
